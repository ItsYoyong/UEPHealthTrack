ABOUT THE CLINIC

OUR MISSION

The University Medical and Dental Clinic is committed to providing accessible, high-quality, and confidential health services to the entire University community. We focus on preventive care, health education, and immediate medical attention to ensure a healthy and safe learning environment.

CLINIC LEADERSHIP

Director, Medical & Dental Health Services Unit

Dr. Mary Sandie Jayme Mahusay - Tan

Leading the University of Eastern Philippines Medical and Dental Health Services Unit with a dedication to public health and student welfare. Dr. Tan ensures that the unit operates effectively to meet the health needs of students, faculty, and staff through professional and compassionate care.

WHO WE ARE

The UEP clinic, known as the UEP Medical and Dental Health Services Unit, is located within the main campus of the University of Eastern Philippines (UEP) in Catarman, Northern Samar. Your primary health and wellness resource on campus, dedicated to supporting student and staff health.
